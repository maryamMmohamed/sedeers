Frequently Asked Questions

What is Sedeers.com?

Sedeers.com is the first online charitable community that connects Non-Profits with their supporters (Volunteers, Donors, and Companies), to achieve goals that serve humanity. In addition to several technology services that we provide to Non-Profits.
- A Non-Profit! You can create and manage your page on our website, and post your need for donations, volunteers, and other activities.
- A Volunteer! You can easily reach Non-Profits and cases that interest you, you can also search for a suitable volunteering opportunities, and receive a “Volunteer Certificate”.
- A Donor! You can follow up Non-Profits, Cases, and Projects that need donations. You can also track the real effect of your donation, and view reports with all your donations and your donation plan.
- A Company! You can track Non-Profits and announce your projects and activities.

What can Sedeers.com provide for Non-Profits?

Sedeers.com provides many services for Non-Profits. Free services and paid services.
* Free services imply:
- Donations Gateway: A service that enables donors to donate online for Non-Profit.
- Sedeers.com Office: A software that enables Non-Profit to manage and publish its content on its page at insanyah.com, and the Non-Profit’s website. It also enables Non-Profit to manage both the donations and voluntary process.
- Sedeers.com Application for mobile.
* Paid services imply:
- Digital marketing strategy.
- Social media management.
- Website development and mobile application development.

How to volunteer through Sedeers.com?

Sign up to Sedeers.com and choose your areas of interest. After that you can browse all Non-Profits on our website. You can also check the available volunteering opportunities, and communicate rapidly with Non-profits.

How to donate through Sedeers.com?

After you Sign up to Sedeers.com, you can donate to Non-profits or to projects through “Donations Gateway” on our website.
* The available donation methods:
- Online: Using Credit Card (Such as: Master Card and Visa Card)
- Cash: Through representative
- In-Kind: Through representative
After donation you will receive a monthly report with the developments occurred by your donation.

Does Sedeers.com collect donations on behalf of Non-Profits?

Sedeers.com doesn’t collect any donations in any way for any Non-Profit.

On what basis does Sedeers.com choose the charities?

- Sedeers.com focuses on the objective of the charity, so the charity must serves a community issue and needs our help and services.
- The charity must be Non-Profitable.
- The Non-Profit should be registered, and its legal papers are valid (That is, the Non-Profit should has a valid “Fund-raising Permit”). If you are a Non-Profit and you want to join us, send an e-mail to: Sharaf@insanyah.com
